Why ponce is very hot?

ponce is located in the south of Puerto Rico and the temperature in ponce frequently is around 27.81 °c to 34.45 °c, hot, oppressive, windy, and partly cloudy. One of the longest zipline!

In the mountains at the center of the Island in the town of Orocovis is a modern marvel of adrenaline-pumping adventure known as “The Monster,” a 2.5-kilometer a zip line that is one of the longest in the world. between rolling mountains covered in dense forest. The area boasts some of the most breathtaking mountain views on the Island, which you’ll see flying beneath you

Arecibo

Arecibo is known as La Villa del Capitán Correa, in memory of a battle fought successfully by Captain Antonio de Los Reyes Correa Arecibo is the largest city in size on the island. Located in the northern Coastal Valley region, Arecibo has some points of interest to visit and enjoy. You can visit La Cueva del Indio drawings of the Indians that lived in this area in the prehistoric Era found in the walls of the cave. You can visit Cueva Ventana ( window cave) a large cave located at the top of a limestone cliff, overlooking the Rio Grande de Arecibo Valley.

Boricua de pura sepa!

"Boricua" means native from Borinquen(which is the original name of Puerto Rico). This is similar to "Puerto Rican" which means native from Puerto Rico Boricuas are people that shared their joy with others, love to laugh, and, help others. they love the music some of them have created a sport called "boseteo" you can receive a hello like "que e la que hay papi!". It does not matter where you're going boricuas will help you to get there. The big population speaks English, You for sure will love their seasoned food one thing they don't love is the Iguanas a reptile imported to Puerto Rico as a pet, but some of the Boricuas stopped caring for them and left them in the forest. Iguanas are all around Puerto Rico.
