Content

Home page

Puerto Rico is a tropical island, the beaches are beautiful and the people known as Boricuas can make your day with their jokes and history. The food is on another level no matter where you are going, but if you pass by a local of 'pinchos' (meat skewer) you must stop over there and try one you will never regret. Despite the storms that have passed through this beautiful island they stand up and recover once again showing resilience and perseverance keeping an outstanding place to visit.

Puerto Rico due to being a tropical Island they have storm season, in 2017 one Hurricane named Maria category 5 was recognized as one of the strongest hurricanes to impact the island causing a major humanitarian crisis and making damage cost of US$90 billion. About Me
